03 — Voice Principles

How CannaSpy speaks

Five rules. Apply them to every screen, email, alert, and empty state.

RULE 01
Direct, not clever
Write exactly what you mean. CannaSpy's buyers are operators — busy, skeptical, and have seen every SaaS pitch. Say the thing clearly.
Wrong"Unlock unprecedented visibility into your competitive landscape"
Right"See every price change your rivals make before you open the doors"
RULE 02
Confident, not arrogant
CannaSpy knows it has something valuable. It doesn't need to oversell it.
Wrong"The most powerful competitive intelligence platform ever built for cannabis"
Right"Real-time competitor pricing and the ability to block rivals from seeing what you see"
RULE 03
Factual about blocking — never threatening
The cancel-block consequence is a product feature, not a threat. State it neutrally. Let the operator draw their own conclusions.
Wrong (UI)"Cancel and we'll immediately call your rivals"
Right (UI)"Canceling this block will re-add [Competitor] to our active prospect list. Our team typically follows up within 24–48 hours."
Critical distinction This language belongs in the product UI — factual and neutral. It does NOT belong in marketing copy or cold outreach emails, where it reads as a threat rather than a feature.
RULE 04
War room, not wellness
CannaSpy is for operators competing hard. The tone is serious, focused, and strategic. Not aggressive — but not warm and soft either.
Wrong"You're all caught up! 🎉 No competitor changes today."
Right"All clear across 10 markets as of 6:42 AM. Last check: 2 minutes ago."
RULE 05
Never write "competitive intelligence platform"
Write "competitive advantage." Intelligence describes the mechanism. Advantage describes the outcome. Operators buy outcomes.
Wrong"CannaSpy is a competitive intelligence platform for dispensary operators"
Right"CannaSpy gives dispensary operators a competitive advantage their rivals can't access"

06 — Typography

Type system

Two typefaces. Two weights. Used consistently across all surfaces.

DM Sans — Primary / Display / Body
First to respond wins the territory.
CannaSpy monitors competitor dispensaries in real time — pricing, promotions, product catalog activity — and delivers that intelligence through a dashboard built for operators who compete hard.
Space Mono — Labels / Numbers / Timestamps / IDs
SCREEN_04 · COMMAND CENTER
Last checked: 06:42:18 UTC-8 · 10 markets active · 3 unreviewed alerts
MedMen West Hollywood · $32.00 → $28.00 · −12.5% · detected 4min ago
Type rules Weights: 400 regular, 500 medium, 600 semibold only. Never 700+ — too heavy against dark surfaces. Numbers and prices always Space Mono. Timestamps always Space Mono, always show local time converted from UTC.
07 — What to Avoid

Never do this

CannaSpy is a professional B2B intelligence tool, not a dispensary brand.

  • Use leaf emoji, cannabis leaf imagery, or plant iconography in the product UI
  • Reference "420", "blaze", "high times", or any cannabis culture slang
  • Use green as a dominant theme color — it reads as a dispensary, not an intelligence platform
  • Write "competitive intelligence platform" — write "competitive advantage"
  • Write "we'll notify your competitors" — write "they'll be re-added to our prospect list"
  • Make the cancel block warning feel punitive — it must be factual and neutral
  • Use "Nothing here yet" or "No data available" as empty states — write operational status
  • Use "Submit" or "OK" as button labels — use specific action verbs
  • Mention the block cancellation consequence in marketing copy or cold outreach — product UI only
  • Use Inter, Roboto, or system fonts — use DM Sans + Space Mono
